Journal de Lyon
ou Mémoires historiques et politiques de ce qui s'est passé de plus remarquable dans la ville de Lyon et dans la province depuis le commencement du XVIIIe jusqu'à présent

Historical Journal of Lyon

  • 7 volumes, curated today by the Musées Gadagne - Lyon
  • Was written between 1715 and 1744
  • Daily life of local bourgeoisie (social, political, cultural aspects)

The author

  • Léonard Michon (1675-1746)
  • Lawyer at the Bureau des finances of Lyon (1700-1746)
  • Administrator of a city hospital (1714-1715)
  • Member of the city council (1721-1722)

Diversity of thematics

Mariage, birth, funerals, sales of offices, etc.

Methodology

XML/TEI : one format to describe them all

  • Text Encoding Initiative : develops and maintains a standard for the representation of texts in digital form (www.tei-c.org)
  • Since 1994, XML/TEI is used by scholars, museum, publishers… for research and preservation of texts
  • XML Files (one per year) stored on an eXist server, readable on a dedicated website http://journal-michon.symogih.org/works/home.html (prototype version)
  • TEI schema to describe structure (paragraphe, division) and elements (dates, named entities)

Collect and analysis of historical informations

  • Using SyMoGIH method
  • PostgreSQL Database : collaborative and accumulative system
